Our Solution

Automated S&C Tracking

The SET-vision system (coming soon) automates barbell-based strength and conditioning training. Enabling coaches with more time to focus on their athletes instead of data management.

Long-Term Performance Analysis

Our system helps overview progression both during training and throughout extended training blocks. With extended use SET-vision aims to help forecast performance changes and optimize training based on metrics extracted directly from athlete performance during training.

Flexible system design

At Strength Edge Tech, we aim to personalise the application of the SET-vision system based on metrics most relevant to your sport-specific S&C training application. We work with coaches to understand how to best apply our tools for their needs. By coaches for coaches.
